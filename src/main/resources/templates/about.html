About Game Encyclopedia

Your ultimate source for video game information and reviews

Our Mission

Game Encyclopedia aims to be a comprehensive resource for gamers to discover, explore, and share their experiences with video games. We strive to provide accurate information, honest reviews, and a platform for gamers to connect and discuss their favorite titles.

Features

  • Comprehensive database of video games across platforms and genres
  • User-submitted reviews and ratings
  • Detailed information about developers, release dates, and genres
  • Search functionality to easily find games
  • Community-driven content and discussions

Our Team

Game Encyclopedia is a project developed by a team of passionate gamers and developers from Tbilisi State University. We welcome contributions from the gaming community to make this resource even better.

If you'd like to get involved or have any questions, please contact us.
